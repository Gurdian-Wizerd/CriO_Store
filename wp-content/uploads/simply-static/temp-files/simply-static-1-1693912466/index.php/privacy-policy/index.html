3RD PARTY

Where your data is going:

We do not deal with any 3rd party services and give your account, order, or any kind of data. Your data is encrypted and stored only on our servers. We want to raise your voice against data theft and selling data to Ad agencies because privacy is about trust.

INCOGNITO

Incognito mode description:

When you turn on the incognito mode in our platform, the products you are visiting, the time you are online, your account informations or any other kind of data is stored. Only when you order in incognito mode, your account and order details are stored.

ALL YOUR DATA

What you can do in All Your Data:

We offer you to see all the data which are about you which are stored on our server and delete them if you want. Only your order information will be stored for legal reasons, but will not be displayed in your account or anywhere on our platform.

LEGAL ISSUES

For any legal issues:

Unfortunately, it is also possible that government can send us any legal notice for the investigation of any kind of illegal activity. In that case, we will verify that notice and inform you about it. Your information can only be shown in court.

OUR TEAM

How much can we do with your data:

Yes, we can see your data, but the sellers and support team can only see your account and order information after getting your order id or all your account information. Not even the technical team can access your data without any legal notice.

DATA COLLECTION

Which data we actually collect:

We collect your location, pages you view, products you see, the amount of time you are spending on each product, notification clicks, and heatmaps. These are stored in a way that they can stay totally disconnected from your account information.
